Birth of Nanak

Nanak was the founder of one of India's religions called Sikhism. Nanak was born in 1469 in the village of Talvandi in Punjab.

Sikhism is a religion founded on the belief in one god and the importance of following strict moral guidelines. Nanak believed that a person could gain freedom from rebirth through meditation. Nanak was known as Guru Nanak later in his life.

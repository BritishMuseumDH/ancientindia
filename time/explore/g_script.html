Indus script used

The distinctive signs of the Indus script appear on seals, pottery, copper tablets and stone. Examples of the Indus script have been found at all Indus Valley sites found so far.

Indus Valley seals and other examples of the Indus script have also been found in Mesopotamia and Afghanistan.

Although it is difficult to date Indus Valley inscriptions, archaeologists believe that the Indus script was no longer used after about 1700 B.C.

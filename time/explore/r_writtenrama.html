Written version of the Ramayana

The Ramayana is an important epic poem. The Ramayana tells the story of a Prince named Rama who battles with an evil demon named Ravana.

After Rama defeats the demon he returns to his home in the city of Ayodhya. This return is celebrated every year by Hindus as the festival of Diwali.

The original version of the Ramayana was composed by the poet Valmiki. It was first composed around the sixth century B.C. However, the Ramayana was written down for the first time in the fifth century A.D.

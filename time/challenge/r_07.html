Nataraja

This is a bronze sculpture of the Hindu god Shiva from south India. It was made around A.D. 1100.

Shiva appears in many ways to reflect the different functions he performs in the universe. One of the most common ways he is shown in sculpture is as Nataraja, the lord of the dance.

Nataraja is both a creator and destroyer. His dance represents the endless cycle of creation, destruction and rebirth which maintains balance in the universe.

When Panini finished his schooling, he decided he would work on making the rules of language clearer so that they would be understandable and correct. Panini knew what he wanted to do. However, he was not sure how to begin his task.

Panini decided that he would start by talking to all of the wise and educated men he could find about the rules of language. He spoke with many wise men who shared his belief that the rules could be made easier and clearer. Then one day Panini met Isvara Deva.

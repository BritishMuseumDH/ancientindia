Panini's book was called the Astadhyayi and was written in about the fourth century B.C. It is a complete grammar of the Sanskrit language. Sanskrit was a language only used by the Brahmins. Panini's work helped to begin the standardization of the language. Scholars today are able to learn many things about what the language was like in Panini's time because he had written his book outlining so much about the structure of the language.

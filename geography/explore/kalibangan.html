Kalibangan

Kalibangan was an early Indus Valley city located next to the Ghaggar River which is now dry. It was first excavated in 1959.

Kalibangan was probably one of the larger Indus Valley cities. It had a similar town plan to that of Mohenjo-daro and Harappa with a raised 'citadel' area and streets organised on a grid plan.

Title:Knife
Excavation number: DK 6287
 
Excavation notes:
This metal blade was found 20 feet and 2 inches below the surface.

Location: 4, -, 15

Other notes:
Metal blades like this are not commonly found at Indus Valley sites. It took a lot of time and skill for Indus Valley craftsmen to create objects out of metal. Thus, metal tools and weapons would probably have been highly valued and owned by important, wealthy or powerful people.

Recent excavations

In the past few years, several teams of archaeologists have been working at Mohenjo-daro. From these excavations, archaeologists have developed interesting ideas about the use and purpose of different areas of the city as well as the role played by the city of Mohenjo-daro within the Indus Valley framework of cities. Modern excavations have also helped us to understand more about the meaning of what was found by the first excavators at the site.

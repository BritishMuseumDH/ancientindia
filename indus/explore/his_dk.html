DK Area

The area known as 'DK' was excavated by Kashinath Narayan Dikshit from 1924-5 and Ernest J.H. Mackay from 1927-31.

DK Area was divided into several different sections, which were each given a letter 'A', 'B', 'C', 'D', 'G'. Dikshit also dug two trenches which were given the letters 'E' and 'F'.

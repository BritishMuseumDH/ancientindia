Dome (lower)

The main part of the Amaravati Stupa was a solid great dome which most likely stood about 18 metres high. The dome was made of pale green limestone which was probably painted with bright colours.

A small box made of stone, pottery or metal was usually placed within the dome of a stupa. This box contained the ashes of the Buddha or an important Buddhist figure.

The lower part of the dome was covered with large stone reliefs.

First Sermon

After achieving Enlightenment, the Buddha preached his First Sermon in a Deer Park. He went on to devote the rest of his life to teaching others how to live a spiritual and meaningful life.

This scene shows the First Sermon of the Buddha. The dharmachakra in the centre of the scene represents the teachings of the Buddha. On either side of the dharmachakra there are people holding up their hands in gestures of worship.

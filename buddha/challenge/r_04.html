Pillar with lion

The Buddha is sometimes represented as a pillar with a lion on top.

The lion is a powerful and majestic beast which represented greatness. The Buddha was sometimes called 'Sakyasimha' which means the lion of the Sakyas. The Sakyas were the royal family that the Buddha was born into.

This is a stone relief from the Great Stupa at Amaravati showing the pillar with the lion on top.

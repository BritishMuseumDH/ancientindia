Kumbha Mela

The Kumbha Mela is one of the major religious festivals for the Hindus. It is held in the months of January and February every 12 years at four sacred sites, Allahabad, Nasik, Ujjain and Kasi. The most important of the Kumbha Melas takes place at Allahabad. Pilgrims bathe in the holy river to wash away their sins.
